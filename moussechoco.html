Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients for 5-6 people

  • 200g of good quality baking chocolate (Nestle or Lindt)
  • 6 big eggs
  • 1tbsp of coffee

Beat the eggs white strongly.
Melt chocolate in bain marie.
Add coffee to the chocolate and the eggs yolks.
Then incorporate the whites to the chocolate.
Pour the mix into several small bowls.
Leave in the fridge for at leat 4-6 hours.
