French Chocolate Cake

  • 200g of dark baking chocolate (Nestle)
  • 4 eggs
  • 100g butter
  • 200g sugar
  • 40g self-raising flour

Heat the oven to 180Â°C.
Melt chocolate and butter together in a pan.
Mix sugar, flour and eggs in a large bowl.
Once chocolate is melted, add it to the mix.
Pour into a springform cake tin.
Bake for 40 minutes.
