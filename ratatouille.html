Ratatouille

  • 2 eggplants
  • 5 courgettes
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • Thyme, oregano
  • Salt, pepper

Cut all the vegetables coarsely. Heat oil in two different pans.
Place the eggplants in the large pan and the courgettes in the medium pan. Cook them separately foe at least 15-20 minutes on low heat.
Then add the courgettes to the eggplants. Mix. Add the tomatoes and herbs and mix again.
Simmer for two hours until the vegetables are very well cooked. Season to taste.
