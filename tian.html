Tian

  • 2 eggplants
  • 3 courgettes
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 red oignon
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Thyme, oregano
  • Salt, pepper

Rinse and cut the vegetables into slices.
Finely slice the red oignon and cook it in a pan with olive oil on low heat until it becomes soft.
Once well cooked, put the oignon in a lasagna dish then place the sliced vegetables vertically in alternative layers.
Salt and pepper. Cover with some herbs and drizzle with oilve oil.
